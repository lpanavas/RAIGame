Welcome to the AI Regulation Game!

The European Union has just passed the AI Act, a significant regulation on AI technology, aimed at ensuring safe and lawful use of AI.

In this game, you'll be required to categorize different uses of AI into three groups:

  1. Never Allowed: These are applications of AI that are completely banned under the AI Act due to potential harm or violation of human rights.
  2. High Risk: These AI applications are not banned, but are subject to strict regulatory requirements due to their potential to cause harm or adverse impact.
  3. Low Risk: These are AI applications that pose minimal risk to individual rights or safety and have less stringent regulatory requirements.

For every correctly placed AI use case, you'll earn points. The base score is 10 points, and you'll earn an additional 2 points for each card already in the bucket. However, if you place a use case in the wrong category, you'll receive a penalty which subtracts from your total score. The base penalty is 20 points, and you'll lose an additional 4 points for each card already in the bucket.

Your objective is to maximize your score by accurately categorizing as many AI use cases as possible. Remember, once you place a card in a bucket, you cannot move it. Choose wisely and good luck!
